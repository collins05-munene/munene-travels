About London

Dive into the rich history, culture, and iconic landmarks of the UK's capital.

London, a global city, is famous for its history, museums, and royal palaces, alongside its modern skyline, bustling markets, and vibrant nightlife. Explore famous spots like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the London Eye.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit London is from April to June or September to November when the weather is mild and there are fewer crowds.

Things to Do in London

  • Visit the Tower of London
  • Take a ride on the London Eye
  • Explore the British Museum
  • See the Changing of the Guard at Buckingham Palace
  • Walk along the Thames River

Travel Tips

  • Get an Oyster Card for affordable public transport
  • Wear comfortable shoes for exploring on foot
  • Book tickets in advance for major attractions to save time
  • Take an afternoon tea at one of London's traditional tea rooms
