About Maasai Mara

The Maasai Mara is one of Kenya's most renowned wildlife reserves, known for its annual Great Migration.

Located in the southwestern part of Kenya, the Maasai Mara is home to a vast array of wildlife, including lions, elephants, and rhinos. The famous Mara River sees the crossing of millions of wildebeests, zebras, and gazelles each year during the migration.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Maasai Mara is during the annual Great Migration from July to October. The dry season between June and October offers ideal conditions for wildlife viewing.

Things to Do in Maasai Mara

  • Take a safari to witness the Big Five
  • Witness the Great Migration
  • Explore the Mara River and see crocodiles
  • Visit Maasai villages and learn about the culture
  • Enjoy hot air balloon safaris over the savannah

Travel Tips

  • Carry a good pair of binoculars for game viewing
  • Wear neutral-colored clothing to blend in with nature
  • Be prepared for early morning game drives
  • Bring a camera to capture the incredible wildlife and scenery
