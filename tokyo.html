About Tokyo

Immerse yourself in the exciting fusion of ancient traditions and modern innovation in Tokyo.

From traditional temples like Senso-ji to the high-tech districts of Shibuya and Shinjuku, Tokyo offers an experience like no other.

Best Time to Visit

Visit Tokyo during the spring (March to May) for the famous cherry blossom season or in the fall (September to November) for the beautiful autumn colors.

Things to Do in Tokyo

  • Visit the Meiji Shrine
  • Explore Akihabara for anime and electronics
  • Enjoy Tokyo Disneyland
  • Shop in Harajuku and Shibuya
  • Take a boat ride in Asakusa

Image Gallery

Travel Tips

  • Learn a few basic Japanese phrases to make communication easier
  • Use the JR Pass for cost-effective travel around Tokyo
  • Expect long queues for popular attractions
  • Bring cash, as some places do not accept credit cards
