About Diani Beach

Diani Beach is one of Kenya's most beautiful and renowned beach destinations, located on the Indian Ocean coastline.

Known for its pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, Diani Beach is the perfect getaway for relaxation, water sports, and exploring nature. The beach is surrounded by lush tropical vegetation, making it a paradise for travelers.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Diani Beach is between December and March, when the weather is hot and dry, perfect for beach activities and watersports.

Things to Do in Diani Beach

  • Relax on the beautiful sandy beaches
  • Snorkel or dive in the coral reefs
  • Explore the Shimba Hills National Reserve
  • Go kite surfing or parasailing
  • Visit the Colobus Conservation Center

Image Gallery

Travel Tips

  • Pack lightweight clothes, sunscreen, and a hat to stay comfortable in the sun
  • Consider renting a car for easy access to different beach spots and nearby attractions
  • Be aware of the tides when swimming or snorkeling
  • Stay hydrated and enjoy local fresh fruit drinks
