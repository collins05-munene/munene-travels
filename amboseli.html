About Amboseli

Amboseli National Park, located at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro, is one of Kenya’s most iconic parks, known for its breathtaking views and large elephant herds.

The park is famous for its stunning landscape, where the vast savannah meets the majestic mountain peaks of Kilimanjaro. Visitors are often treated to spectacular wildlife sightings, especially elephants, lions, and cheetahs.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Amboseli is during the dry season from June to October when wildlife is easier to spot due to the sparse vegetation and animals gather around water sources.

Things to Do in Amboseli

  • Game drives to spot elephants and other wildlife
  • Visit the observation hill for stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro
  • Explore the wetlands area for birdwatching
  • Take a guided walk with Maasai guides
  • Visit the Maasai villages to experience local culture

Travel Tips

  • Carry plenty of water and sunscreen as the area can get quite hot
  • Wear neutral-colored clothing for safaris
  • Bring binoculars and a camera to capture wildlife moments
  • Respect Maasai culture and traditions if visiting villages
